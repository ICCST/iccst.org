16th ICCST to convene in Halle (Saale), Germany

The members of the International Advisory Board are pleased to announce the event of the 16th ICCST in Halle (Saale), Germany. The local organization will be carried out by Professor Wessjohann and his team at the Leibniz Institute of Plant Biochemistry.
