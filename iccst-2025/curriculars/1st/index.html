16. International Conference on the Chemistry of Selenium and Tellurium, ICCST-2025

It is our great pleasure to announce the 16th  International Conference on the Chemistry of Selenium and Tellurium, ICCST-16, which will take place on
August 26 - 29, 2025

in the National Academy of Sciences Leopoldina, in Halle (Saale), Germany. 

The event is being organized by Prof. Ludger Wessjohann and his team from the Leibniz Institute of Plant Biochemistry in Halle.

The ICCST-16 will continue the tradition of previous conferences, offering a scientific program on the topics:
 
  • Organic and inorganic chemistry of higher chalcogens
  • Selenium in agriculture and nutrition
  • Selenium in biochemistry, biology and medicinal sciences
  • Higher chalcogenes in material sciences
