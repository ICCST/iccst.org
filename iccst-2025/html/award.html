Wolfgang Günther Prize

In memory of Wolfgang Günther, the founder of the ICCST conference series , we are awarding the Wolfgang Günther Prize, endowed with 500 euros, at the 16th ICCST. The prize winner should have made a special contribution to selenium or tellurium chemistry.

The organizers have received nine nominations in total. The International Advisory Board will vote on the awarding of the price in two categories: Lifetime Achievements and Scientific Excellence.

The winners will be announced at the conference.
