Wolfgang Günther Prize

In memory of Wolfgang Günther, the founder of the ICCST conference series , we are awarding the Wolfgang Günther Prize, endowed with 500 euros, at the 16th ICCST. The prize winner should have made a special contribution to selenium or tellurium chemistry. The organizing committee is calling for nominations for the award.
