Wolfgang Günther Prize

In memory of Wolfgang Günther, the founder of the ICCST conference series , we are awarding the Wolfgang Günther Prize, endowed with 500 euros, at the 16th ICCST. The prize winner should have made a special contribution to selenium or tellurium chemistry. The organizing committee is calling for nominations for the award. The deadline for nominations is spring 2025 (the exact date will be announced later).

The International Advisory Board will review the nominations and award the price.
