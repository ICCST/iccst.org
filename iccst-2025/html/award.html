Wolfgang Günther Prize

In memory of Wolfgang Günther, the founder of the ICCST conference series , we are awarding the Wolfgang Günther Prize, endowed with EUR 500, a silver medal (see picture), and conference participation support. The award is supported by the Gunther family and conference sponsors.

The award has two categories: The Lifetime Achievement and the Scientific Excellence Award in Se/Te-Chemistry (and related sciences). All scientist active in the ICCST area can nominate candidates. The ICCST-IAB (International Advisory Board) will vote and decide for the winners, which will be honored during the conference (Fri 29. Aug. 2025).

This year's winners are:

  1. Prof. Dr. Tristram Chivers, Univ. of Calgary (Emeritus), Calgary, Canada - Lifetime Achievement
  2. Prof. Dr. Jens Beckmann, Univ. Bremen, Bremen, Germany - Scientific Excellence in Se/Te-Chemistry (and related sciences)

Best short talk prize

A committee of selected senior participants will select the best short talks given during the conference. The best three ones will receive a certificate. The selected best presenter will receive a price money of EUR 100 (Only if present at the ceremony, otherwise prize money goes to subsequent place! In case of equal votes, prize can be split.)
