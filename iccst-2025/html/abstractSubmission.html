Abstract Submission

Please use either of the following templates for the submission of your abstract

Poster

There will be no poster session. Instead, we will have short talks available for both students and experienced researchers. Thus, please apply by sending us a short talk abstract.

Short talks

Short talks will be available in large number and will substitute posters. The exact time of your short talk will be announced after the selection and the number is known. Total time will be between 6 and 10 minutes and should be constrained to ca. 5 slides. Details will follow end of June. Selected short talks will receive an extended talk time (10-15 min.) and selected speakers will be informed individually. English only. Short talk abstracts are limited to one page!!

Please download your abstract template here: ICCST-16_Short_talk_abstract_template.docx
Send send it to abstract@iccst.org. Deadline (extended) is June 16, 2025.

Invited talks

These are on invitation only, following suggestions from the board members. If you wish to give an invited talk, please ask a board member or Professor Wessjohann (info@iccst.org) for consideration. Selected invited talks will receive keynote or plenary status and thus extended presentation time.

Here is the template for invited talks: ICCST-16_Invited_talk_abstract_template.docx
Invited speakers please also send a picture and a CV along the abstract to: abstract@iccst.org
