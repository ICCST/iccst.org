16th ICCST to convene in Halle (Saale), Germany

The members of the International Advisory Board are pleased to announce the event of the 16th ICCST in Halle (Saale), Germany. The local organization will be carried out by Professor Wessjohann and his team at the Leibniz Institute of Plant Biochemistry. This page will be updated, as further information becomes available.

Date

August 26 - 29, 2025 (Tue - Fri)

Please register

Registration and abstract submission is now open. For details, please see our registration and abstract submission pages.

Topics of the conference:

  • Organic chemistry of higher chalcogens
  • Inorganic chemistry of higher chalcogenes
  • Selenium in Agriculture and Nutrition
  • Selenium in Biochemistry, Biology and Medicinal Sciences
  • Higher Chalcogenes in Material Science
