Conference Proceedings Special Issue

There will be a special opportunity to publish your Selenium, Tellurium and related research in a special issue of the joint proceedings of WSeS-12 & ICCST-16 in Phosphorus, Sulfur, and Silicon and the Related Elements (PSSi) by Taylor & Francis Publisher. The manuscripts will be fully peer reviewed by experts in the field, thus acceptance of your publication is of course pending acceptance by the editors and reviewers.
