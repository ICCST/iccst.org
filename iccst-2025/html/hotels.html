Hotels

Many different hotels located close to the city center cater to different comfort requirements. These include hotels with moderate accommodation prices, which are recommended for those on a tight travel budget.

For general cost planning and booking, you might want to consult web sites like booking.com , Expedia , Trivago , HRS , etc. or hotels or booking sites offered by your preferred search engine. Tip: Be careful not to confuse Halle (Saale) in Saxony-Anhalt with other cities of the same name.

We recommend to stay a night longer in Halle and visit one of Germanys biggest fun fairs (Lantern festival , Aug 29-31) along the Saale river, with multiple folkloristic and fun events, fireworks and more. We strongly advise you to book your hotel as early as possible, as the rooms may be fully booked due to the fun fair.
