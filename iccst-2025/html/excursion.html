Conference Trip

Medieval Villages, Castles and Harz Mountains
full day bus tour, 90 € (approx. 93 US$)
(booked participants only - late participation is pending availability)

The conference trip will go to the Harz mountain area, where most of the early Selenium ores have been discovered and exported, e.g. to Berzelius in Sweden, for experiments. You will visit Wernigerode with its castle and stroll through the medieval heart of the small town, explore the Hermann cave, drive through the mountains and over the Rappbode dam, the biggest in Germany before returning to join the conference dinner.
