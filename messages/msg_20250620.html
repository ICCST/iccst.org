The panel of speakers at the 16th ICCST is nearly complete
June 2025

The organization of the 16th ICCST is entering its final phase. Local organization will be carried out by Professor Wessjohann and his team at the Leibniz Institute of Plant Biochemistry. The conference will be held from

August 26 - 29, 2025 (Tue - Fri)
in Halle (Saale), Germany
It features a rich set of talks, which cover all fields of the conference topics:
  • Organic chemistry of higher chalcogens
  • Inorganic chemistry of higher chalcogenes
  • Selenium in Agriculture and Nutrition
  • Selenium in Biochemistry, Biology and Medicinal Sciences
  • Higher Chalcogenes in Material Science

Registration is still possible. Please visit our registration page or proceed directly to our service provider https://eveeno.com/iccst-2025 . For further details, please refer to our conference website. The members of the International Advisory Board and local organizers are looking forward to welcome you in Halle.

Venue

The conference will be held on the premises of the German National Academy of Sciences Leopoldina .
