About ICCST

The International Conferences on the Chemistry of Selenium and Tellurium are a series of international conferences held triennially. The web page for this conference series is currently maintained by Professor Wessjohanns group at the

Leibniz Institute of Plant Biochemistry (IPB)

06120 Halle (Saale)
