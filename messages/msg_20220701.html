July 2022

Registration for the ICCST-15 is now open

The 15th International Conference on the Chemistry of Selenium and Tellurium (ICCST-15) will be held in Brazil, Florianópolis, state of Santa Catarina from Nov. 28. – Dec. 2. 2022 at the Hotel Jurerê Beach Village under the auspices of Prof. Dr. Antonio Luiz Braga. The conference is dedicated to Dr. Wolfgang Günther, the late founder of the conference series.

Early Bird Registration is now open. The program will include Plenary, Invited Lectures, and Oral Communications. A list of the confirmed speakers is available at the following link: https://www.iccst15.com.br/conteudo/65 . Additional information can be found in the conference flyer. Further distribution of the flyer among your colleagues and students is highly welcome.

Flash presentations will be selected from the abstract submissions. Details for Registration, Abstract Submission, and Hotel Room Reservation at the Conference Site (Hotel Jurerê Beach Village) are available on the official website of the Conference: https://www.iccst15.com.br/ .

Important Dates:

  • Early-bird Registration Deadline: August 31st, 2022
  • Late Registration: after August 31st, 2022
  • Poster Abstract Submission deadline is September 30th, 2022
  • Poster Abstract Acceptance Notification: October 25th, 2022

Hotel Reservation
A discount of 10% compared to the prices on the hotel page  is available with promo code "ICCST15". Please send an email with PROMOCODE "ICCST15" to reservas@jurere.com.br to request the discount.

We hope you will be able to join this event and enjoy the stimulating scientific program. We strongly encourage the participation of young scientists who will have the opportunity to actively disseminate their results in Flash and Poster Presentations.

We look forward to seeing you in Florianópolis (Brazil) in November 2022. Please follow the conference website  for more information and updates.
