16th ICCST to convene in Halle (Saale), Germany
Feb 2025

The members of the International Advisory Board are pleased to announce the event of the 16th ICCST in Halle (Saale), Germany. The local organization will be carried out by Professor Wessjohann and his team at the Leibniz Institute of Plant Biochemistry. Further information is available on our dedicated conference website.

Registration now open

Registration and payment will be handled by the service provider Eveeno.com. For further information, please refer to our conference website or proceed directly to the Eveeno.com registration page:

https://eveeno.com/iccst-2025 

Date

August 26 - 29, 2025 (Tue - Fri)

The deadline for the submission of abstracts for short talks has been extended to June 16, 2025.

Topics of the conference

  • Organic chemistry of higher chalcogens
  • Inorganic chemistry of higher chalcogenes
  • Selenium in Agriculture and Nutrition
  • Selenium in Biochemistry, Biology and Medicinal Sciences
  • Higher Chalcogenes in Material Science

Venue

The conference will be held on the premises of the German National Academy of Sciences Leopoldina .
